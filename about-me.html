Background

My coding journey started in fall of 2015. Being a big fan of modern technology, of its constant and rapid development along with being attracted by the inside story of how things are done, it was a natural move for me to try myself out in coding.

I enjoy being a lifetime lerner, love creating something out of nothing by mixing my knowledge, skills and imagination. The more days I saw behind while learning to code, the more I fell in love with the new occupation I chose to do for a living. I was lucky enough to meet some great people online, who became my friends and mentors. Being guided and supported by people who have been successfully working in the industry helped me grow faster as the web developer.

I chose Front End Development because it allows me to work closely with design. Having background in photography and digital illustrations I am pulled towards visual aspect of web development. It's amazing to be able to take a design and turn it into a functional product for people to use and enjoy.
