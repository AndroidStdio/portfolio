Coding

My projects are mostly built with plain HTML, CSS and Vanilla JavaScript. Having knowledge on Bootstrap and jQuery, I choose to develop my web pages and apps without relying on frameworks or labraries. The reason is that I only worked on small size projects which benefit the most from a straight forward set up.

I enjoy the process of building web products from scratch. It's an amazing journey of discovery, knowledge evolution and varification of the skills set. Web development is a creative process which often pushes me leaving my comfort zone and this is when we are growing, aren't we?

All my projects are fully responsive. Turning my work into a good user experience on mobile devices is my priority and I take a great care of it. These days mobile pnone is the king and we have to accept it.

Background

My coding journey started in fall of 2015. Being a big fan of modern technology, of its constant and rapid development along with being attracted by the inside story of how things are done, it was a natural move for me to try myself out in coding.

I enjoy being a lifetime learner, love creating something out of nothing by mixing my knowledge, skills and imagination. The more days I see behind while learning to code, the more I fall in love with the new occupation I chose to do for a living. I am lucky enough to meet some great people online, who became my friends and mentors. Being guided and supported by people who have been successfully working in the industry helped me grow faster as the web developer.

I chose Front End Development because it allows me to work closely with design. Having background in photography and digital illustrations I am pulled towards visual aspect of web development. It's amazing to be able to take a design and turn it into a functional product for people to use and enjoy.
