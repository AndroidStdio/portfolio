Livet

Bootstrap based custom website template

Live Demo GitHub

Project description

Livet is a Bootstrap based HTML website template. It was designed to favour a media agency or a startup with a young spirit and positive energy, who want to create their online presence.

This template is focused on its visitors. At the same time giving the agency an opportunity to present themselves in the best possible way to convert the website visitors into potential clients.

Template is easly customisable, well documented and fully responsive. This way even users with little or no coding experiemce could effortlessly make adjastments to the template to meet their individual needs.

My Role

I needed to design and develop a website template to show an agency's potential clients their work and provide website visitors with a convenient way to contact the team.

In addition it was necessary to write a detailed documentation for the template, so that the customization of it was an enjoyable experience.

Template had to fit well on different devices regardles of width of height.

Details

Enjoyable user experience was my priority when working on the template. I wanted to make sure the website is easy to navigate, has clear visual hierarchy and has familiar patterns throughout the page.

Working on Livet template was a great learning process for me. I managed to design and develop a template from scratch which certainly advanced my skills and understanding of web design.
